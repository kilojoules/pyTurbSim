DOE
The Department of Energy is the United States Agency ‘dedicated to advancing energy technology and promoting related innovation’.
input file
config file
TurbSim input/config files specify the input parameters which control TurbSim output. These files are the exclusive method for controlling output from O-TurbSim. PyTurbSim has implemented additional methods (described in the Usage section).
NREL
The National Renewable Energy Lab is a federally funded research and development center (FFRDC) of the DOE.
NWTC
The National Wind Technology Center is a research center at NREL.
O-TurbSim
This refers to the original, Atmospheric version of TurbSim, developed at the NWTC.
